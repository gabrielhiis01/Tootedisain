The Chrome Case begins as a solid billet of ZA-8 zinc-aluminium alloy — chosen over stainless steel for its superior surface density and chromatic response to polishing. Over four hours of four-axis CNC milling at ±0.01mm tolerance, the billet is hollowed to receive your AirPods Pro with a fit that requires no rubber trim and no compromise.

Then it goes to the polishing bench. Six hours per case, moving through twelve grit stages from 80 to 8,000, until the surface crosses a threshold — it stops looking like polished metal and starts looking like liquid. This is not a coating. The reflectivity is structural, built into the alloy itself. It cannot peel, chip, or cloud.

The interior is lined with 2mm of custom-compounded silicone — impact-absorbing without adding bulk. The lid closes with a 400g magnetic snap. Wireless charging works with the lid open. Every case passes a 14-point quality check before it leaves our workshop. Those that fall short go back to the polishing bench — not to a discount bin.

The Chrome Case is available in three independent fits: AirPods Pro 2 (2nd generation), AirPods Pro 1 (1st generation), and AirPods 3. Each version is machined separately to ±0.01mm — there are no universal inserts or adapters. Select your fit above before adding to cart.
The alloy surface is harder than most surfaces it encounters daily — keys, coins, other metals won't mark it. Deep scratches require contact with harder materials such as gemstones or carbide tools. All surface wear is covered by the Lifetime Polish Guarantee: we re-polish your case to factory condition, once per year, for the life of the product.
