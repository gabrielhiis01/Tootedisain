Legal

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 January 2026. Last updated: 1 January 2026.

Overview

We collect what we need. Nothing more.

Liquisity Ltd ("Liquisity", "we", "us") operates liquisity.com. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data Collected

What we collect.

  • Order data — name, shipping address, email address, and order details when you place an order
  • Payment data — processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details
  • Contact data — name, email, and message content when you contact us
  • Newsletter data — email address when you subscribe to product updates
  • Usage data — IP address, browser type, pages visited, collected anonymously via our hosting provider

Use of Data

How we use it.

  • Fulfil and ship your order, and communicate about its status
  • Respond to support and contact enquiries
  • Send product update emails to newsletter subscribers (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax records, fraud prevention)

We do not use your data for automated profiling or advertising targeting.

Third Parties

Who we share data with.

  • Stripe — payment processing. Your card data goes directly to Stripe and is governed by their privacy policy
  • DHL — your name and shipping address to fulfil delivery
  • Email service provider — for sending order confirmations and newsletter emails

We do not share data with any other third parties. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Retention

How long we keep it.

Order data is retained for 7 years for legal and tax compliance. Contact messages are retained for 2 years. Newsletter subscriptions are retained until you unsubscribe. Usage logs are anonymised after 90 days.

Your Rights

GDPR & CCPA rights.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or export the personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@liquisity.com with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

  • Right to access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to opt out — unsubscribe from marketing at any time via the link in any email

Cookies

Cookies.

We use only functional cookies necessary for the site to operate (e.g. your shopping cart). We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies that require consent. No cookie banner is displayed because none is legally required.

Contact

Questions about this policy.
