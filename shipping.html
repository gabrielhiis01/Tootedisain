Policies

Shipping & Returns

Every order is made to order and ships DHL Express. Free worldwide, no minimum.

Processing

Made to order. Ships in 2–5 business days.

Every Liquisity case is machined and hand-polished after your order is placed. We do not hold pre-polished stock. This is not a limitation — it is how we guarantee that every surface reaches our standard before it leaves the workshop.

Processing time is 2–5 business days from order confirmation. You will receive a shipping notification with tracking as soon as your case is dispatched.

Current lead time

2–5 business days. During peak periods (November–December) this may extend to 6–8 business days. We will communicate any delays by email.

Delivery

DHL Express. Free worldwide.

All orders ship via DHL Express with full tracking. Delivery is free to every country — no minimum order, no hidden fees.

  • United Kingdom & Europe: 1–2 business days after dispatch
  • United States & Canada: 2–3 business days after dispatch
  • Australia & Asia-Pacific: 2–4 business days after dispatch
  • Rest of world: 3–5 business days after dispatch

Import duties and local taxes are the responsibility of the recipient and vary by country. We are not able to mark orders as gifts or undervalue shipments.

Returns

30-day returns. No questions asked.

If you are not satisfied with your order, return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund of the product price. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

To initiate a return, email support@liquisity.com with your order number. We will send a return label within one business day. Once the item is received and inspected, your refund will be processed within 5 business days.

  • Items must be returned in original condition and original packaging
  • Cases showing signs of use beyond inspection may be subject to a restocking assessment
  • Personalised or engraved orders are non-returnable

Guarantee

Lifetime Polish Guarantee.

If your Liquisity case ever loses its mirror finish through normal use — not misuse, not impact damage — we will re-polish it to factory standard, free of charge, once per year for the life of the product.

  • You cover shipping to our workshop; we cover the return
  • No registration required. Keep your order confirmation as proof of purchase
  • No expiry date on the guarantee
  • Covers surface finish only, not structural damage or impact dents

To claim, email support@liquisity.com with your order number and a brief description of the issue.

Questions

Still need help?

Email support@liquisity.com or use the contact form. Every message is read and answered by a real person within 24 hours on business days.
